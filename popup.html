Auto-draft replies

When on, opening any conversation generates your decline reply straight into the message box — review and hit Send. It only fills an empty box, so it never overwrites something you've typed, and it drafts each thread once per browsing session. Nothing is ever sent automatically.

Use {{firstName}} to insert the recipient's first name. Shortcut: ⌘⇧L / Ctrl+Shift+L.
